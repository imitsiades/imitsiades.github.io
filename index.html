Did you know

The Romans were the first to make an early version of the modern day pancake using flour, milk,
eggs, and spices. They used honey instead of syrup to flavor them. The Ancient Greeks and the
Elizabethans also ate pancakes

Simple pancake recipe

1 cup of flour
2 tablespoons of white sugar
2 teaspoons of baking powder
1 teaspoon of salt
1 beaten egg
1 cup of milk
2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
Mix everything together and pour it on to a pan and flip after a while (more specific instructions here)
